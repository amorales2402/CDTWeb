About Us

CDT Big Data & Business Intelligence is an information administration consulting firm that manages the sales, inventory, and distribution processes and information of our clients.

The company is composed of professionals with top experience in sales, distribution, information management and technology. Our purpose is to serve as a business partner through the development of top-notch competitive advantages that differentiate each and every one of our clients from its competitors.

CDT’s main objective is to establish processes and controls through the development of world-class technological tools that provide a clear perspective of coverage, positioning, share, and opportunities for a product or brand within a specific market. These will help increase sales in a more effective and efficient manner; ultimately delivering accurate and up-to-date information for precise decision making and commercial action.

Based out of San José, Costa Rica; since 2010 CDT has been a pioneer in its area with vast experience across Latin America and the Caribbean, working closely with renowned transnational firms and constantly striving to expand its business across new borders
