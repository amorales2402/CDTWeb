Who we are?

CDT Big Data & Business Intelligence is a consulting and administration firm for distribution and sales business processes, focused on business intelligence and business information management.

The company is composed of professionals with high experience in distribution, logistics, information management and technology related to the commercial area. This is intended to become a business partner of its customers through the development of competitive advantages that differentiate them from their competitors.

The main objective of CDT is to establish controls for its customers through the development of world-class technological tools that provide accurate and up-to-date information for decision making, commercial actions and greater efficiency and effectiveness of its management.

With this, consultancies, plans and strategies are developed that guarantee the success to the clients based on the definition, monitoring and optimization of their processes and key performance indicators (KPIs).

From its beginnings in 2010 in San José, Costa Rica; CDT has been a pioneer in this area and today has vast experience working closely with renowned transnational firms throughout Latin America and the Caribbean.
